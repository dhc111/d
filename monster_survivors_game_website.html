Game Controls

Movement:

Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move your character

Attacks happen automatically when monsters are in range

Press Space or click to select upgrades when leveling up

Pause:

Press ESC or P to pause the game

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face endless waves of increasingly difficult monsters. Choose your character, collect experience gems to level up, and select powerful abilities and weapons to extend your survival time.

The game features a variety of unique characters, each with their own starting abilities and strengths. As you progress, you'll unlock new characters and discover different strategies to maximize your survival time.

With procedurally generated enemies and a dynamic difficulty curve, every playthrough offers a fresh challenge. How long can you survive the monster onslaught?

Tips & Strategies

  • Focus on movement and positioning - staying alive is more important than attacking
  • Plan your upgrades to create synergies between different abilities
  • Collect experience gems quickly before they disappear
  • Try to funnel enemies into tight groups to maximize area damage effects
  • Prioritize health and defensive upgrades if you're struggling to survive
  • Different characters excel with different weapon types - experiment to find optimal builds
